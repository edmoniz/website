Visualizing the Final Image

There images are my own creations and all rights are retained and reserved.

If you watch to the Ansel Adams videos on the Resorces page you will

hear him refer to "Visualizaition". This was a strong part of Ansel's skill, his ability to look at a scene in front of hme and visualize the final print.

That technique was used here to capture this image and to allow flexibilty during editing. Knowing that the two Y shapes would be a key element it was necessary to leave enough room around them. This a key concept, not to over crop in camera to leave yourself room to work during editing. Again here the image was exposed to the right and the original image has a slight over exposure look to it.

This image did not require a significant amount of post editing. Simple black and white conversion, cropping, global adjustments and some minor local editing for hightlghts.
