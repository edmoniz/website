Brooks Jensen

An institution in American photography. Long known for publishing "Lenswork", originally a quarterly anthology of black and white photography, to now hosting an online version. Brooks was my inspiration for the folio ideas on this website.

Hudon River School

Painters have practiced their skills for hundreds of years constantly refining how to depict the world around us. As photographers we can learn much from their use of form, colour and mood. These same techiniques can be applied to our photography.
