PPOC Salon Submissions

Professional Photographers of Canada (PPOC) hold annual solons both National and Regional. Each participant can submit four images and these are judged by a panel of five judges and umpire. Images are deemed to be "Accepted", "Merit", or "Excellance". Images that recive a minimum of accepted are "hung in salon". These traditions follow from the days of painters salons held in major centers around the world.
